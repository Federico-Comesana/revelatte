Revelatte

Revelatte is the best place to share and enjoy the most awesome public announcements on the Internet. Entertain, inspire and amaze people around the world with funny, heartwarming or shocking content in a few seconds.

Browse public directory Start using it now!

How does it work?

Write something, pick an image, video, link or whatever is on your mind. Set a revelation date and hit submit. A link is generated and you can share it around the internet. The content of your revelation is displayed only after the date set has passed, otherwise it will show a countdown. Simple right? Registration is not required!

Who is using it?

People

A revelation is the best way to tell people you are expecting a new family member, getting out of the closet or revealing stuff people don't know about you. It's easy and fun to use. You should give it a try!

Companies

How about the secret location of your next underground event? Revelatte helps business owners to reveal new products, special offers and keep their customers thrilled with new features.

Futurists

Think you can tell the future? Already know who is going to win the elections? Create a new revelation right now and prove it to your friends.
